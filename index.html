Our Project

How our project works

Inject, from the start, has designed technology with security in mind. Our system makes it incredibly sim ple for law enforcement to catch wanted criminals with our state of the art detection system. Your own store never has to worry about getting robbed ever again, with our technology enabling crime detection in real time, and integrating everything with law enforcement to create a way to instantly catch criminals. These technological innovations will help drastically reduce crimes against local stores and shops, and makes it very easy and simple to instantly make your business a lot safer!.

Why we made this our project

India alone has about one burglary, robbery, or break-in every three minutes. Our team, Inject, realised the urgency of the situation and set out to create a prototype to put a stop to it all. As a result, we developed our security system to significantly improve the problem of security. Most security systems are expensive and difficult to maintain, and our objective at Inject was to create a successful prototype that was not only cost effective but also efficient. If retailers implement these basic adjustments in conjunction with technological developments, society may vastly increase social security.
