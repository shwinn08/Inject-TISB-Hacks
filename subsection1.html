Our Prototypes

Image recognition

Our image recognition system greatly enhances place security. Despite the fact that many stores have cameras to monitor their operations, only around 1 - 2 percent of stores have proper security systems with image recognition due to the high cost and maintenance requirements. Our image recognition technology can recognize wanted criminals, frequent shoppers, and tell the difference between thieves and ordinary shoppers

Receipt Scanner

To increase security in areas where a bill invoice is supplied, we designed a receipt scanner that generates a unique barcode, and you must scan the receipt to depart the shop, which will activate the rotating door. This reduces the number of shoplifters and stolen goods, making it an extremely successful technique.

Emergency calling

The emergency calling feature is highly significant because if a wanted criminal from the database is seen through the camera and identified by image recognition, it immediately contacts 911 so that no time is wasted and cops arrive quickly to ensure that no robbery or crime occurs.
